Proportional (Kp)

Drives the system towards the target based on current error. High Kp causes overshoot. 1.0

Integral (Ki)

Eliminates steady-state error by accumulating past error, but can induce oscillation. 0.0

Derivative (Kd)

Predicts future error. Dampens oscillations to stabilize the system. 0.5
